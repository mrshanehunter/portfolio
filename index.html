Introduction

Most marketing departments usually outsource the creative elements to a third party. Depending on the project, that could be an advertising, design or digital agency. That adds cost and complexity to a project, pushing out timelines. All too often it requires the client to make compromises around strategy & vision.

Working with a “brand is king” mindset, That Guy From Marketing® doesn’t hand off the creative components of any project to an “agency”. Why incur additional cost to slow a project down, knowing you’re going to have to make adjustments to already agreed strategic considerations?

Using the latest versions of Abobe’s Illustrator, InDesign, Photoshop, Dimensions and Premiere Pro, coupled with over 15 years of experience developing strategies and directing creatives, That Guy From Marketing® keeps costs low & projects on track by executing creative internally.

This portfolio was created explicitly to showcase examples of the creative executed by That Guy From Marketing®. A broad sample, it covers brand identity and logo design, branded collateral, social media, packaging design, advertising – print, digital and radio. For more detail on the overarching strategies, visit the main site.
